Privacy Policy

Last Stand Service
Effective Date: February 1, 2026
Last Updated: February 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you call a plumbing business using our SMS response service, we collect:

  • Phone Number: Your mobile phone number (from Caller ID)
  • SMS Messages: The content of text messages you send in response to our automated system
  • Service Details: Information about your plumbing issue, location (ZIP code), and scheduling preferences
  • Metadata: Date, time, and conversation state

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Facilitate communication between you and the plumbing business
  • Notify the business owner of your service request
  • Coordinate emergency vs. non-urgent responses
  • Improve our service quality

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We share your information only with:

  • The plumbing business you contacted: Your phone number, message content, and service details are provided to the business owner
  • Service providers: Twilio (SMS delivery infrastructure)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or legal process

4. Data Retention

We retain your conversation data for:

  • Active conversations: Until service is completed (typically 7 days)
  • Historical records: Up to 90 days for quality assurance and billing
  • Opt-out requests: Permanently, to honor your request

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe
  • Access: Request a copy of your data by emailing support@laststandservice.com
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (we will retain only what's legally required)

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure database storage
  • Limited access controls
  • Regular security audits

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
