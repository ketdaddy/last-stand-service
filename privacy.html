Privacy Policy

Last Stand Service — Last updated: February 11, 2026

1. Overview

Last Stand Service ("we," "us," "our") provides an automated SMS response service for plumbing businesses in San Antonio, Texas. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our SMS service.

2. Information We Collect

When you call a plumbing business that uses our service and the call is not answered, we collect the following information through our automated SMS system:

  • Phone number: Your caller ID from the missed call
  • Message content: Text messages you send in response to our SMS
  • Service details: Information you provide about your plumbing issue (issue description, address, ZIP code, scheduling preference)
  • Language preference: Whether you respond in English or Spanish

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Respond to your missed call with relevant service information
  • Collect details about your plumbing issue to relay to the business
  • Notify the plumbing business so they can follow up with you
  • Improve our service quality and reliability

We will never share, sell, or distribute your mobile phone number, message content, or opt-in data to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Your mobile information is used exclusively to provide the SMS service you initiated by calling the plumbing business.

No mobile opt-in data or phone numbers will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

4. SMS Service Details

  • Service type: Transactional SMS replies to missed calls — not marketing
  • Message frequency: 2–5 text messages per service request only. No recurring messages.
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe from all SMS communications
  • Help: Reply HELP to any message for assistance
  • Rates: Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan

5. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to complete your service request and for our business records. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

6. Data Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • The plumbing business you called: So they can follow up on your service request
  • Service providers: Third-party providers that help us operate the SMS service, including SMS delivery and message processing. These providers process data solely to operate the service and are bound by their own privacy policies.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any other third parties for any purpose, including marketing or promotional purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Request information about what data we have collected about you
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
