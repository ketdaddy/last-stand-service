When customers call and you can't answer, they call the next plumber. Last Stand Service sends an immediate text message on your behalf — collecting their issue, location, and preference so you can follow up with full context.

How It Works

  1. Customer calls your business number — you can't answer
  2. Our system texts them immediately, identifying your business by name
  3. The customer replies with their plumbing issue and details
  4. You receive a notification with everything you need to call them back

Required SMS Disclosure for Your Business

After enrollment, you are required to display the following disclosure on your website, Google Business listing, or anywhere your phone number is published:

"If we miss your call, you may receive a text message to assist with your service request. Message frequency: 2–5 messages per request. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance. Privacy Policy · Terms of Service"

This disclosure ensures your customers are informed before they call, consistent with CTIA guidelines and carrier requirements for transactional SMS.
