# Last Stand Service

## SMS Consent & Privacy Policy

### How SMS Communication Works

Last Stand Service provides automated SMS responses when customers call our plumbing service partners and we're unable to answer immediately.

### Consent to Receive Messages

By calling our business phone number, you consent to receive text message responses from us. This includes:

- Initial response acknowledging your missed call
- Questions about the nature of your plumbing issue
- Emergency response coordination
- Appointment scheduling confirmations
- Service updates related to your request

### Your Rights

- **Opt-Out:** Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe
- **Help:** Reply HELP for assistance
- **Frequency:** You'll only receive messages related to your specific service request
- **Cost:** Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile plan

### Data Privacy

We do not share your phone number or message content with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used solely to coordinate plumbing services.

### Contact

Questions about our SMS service? Call us at the number you originally dialed or email support@laststandservice.com
